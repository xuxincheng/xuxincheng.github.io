Hello, and welcome.

This is the inaugural post of **Xu's Log**, a place where I'll be documenting my learning notes
on artificial intelligence, machine learning, software engineering, and a handful of other
topics I find interesting along the way.

## Why this blog

I've found that the best way to truly understand something is to explain it — to a friend, to a
rubber duck, or, in this case, to a public archive. Writing forces me to:

- Identify the gaps in my own understanding
- Distill noisy reading into a coherent narrative
- Build a personal reference I can come back to in six months

If anything here turns out to be useful to you too, that's a bonus.

## What to expect

Posts here will tend to be **long-form**, somewhere between a tutorial and a survey. I lean on
diagrams, equations, and code snippets when they help — not for decoration. Topics I expect to
cover include:

- LLM training, fine-tuning, and alignment
- Reasoning, tool use, and AI agents
- Diffusion and other generative models
- Engineering practices for ML systems

## Subscribe

There's an [RSS feed](/index.xml) if you'd like to follow along. No newsletter, no tracking —
just plain old syndication.

Thanks for stopping by.
