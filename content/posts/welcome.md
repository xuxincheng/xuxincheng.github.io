Hello, world.

This is the very first post on this blog. Below is some filler content so the
table-of-contents sidebar (visible on wide screens, top-collapsible on narrow
ones) has something to scroll through.

## Why this blog exists

A place to think out loud about AI, LLMs, deep learning, management, finance,
life, and philosophy — written for myself first, shared in case it helps.

### What you'll find here

Long-form notes, half-baked ideas, and the occasional rabbit hole. Nothing here
is meant to be authoritative; it's a working notebook in public.

### What you won't find here

Clickbait, listicles, or "10 things every AI engineer must know." Not my style.

## How posts are organized

Each post has tags (no categories). Use the **Tags** menu to browse by topic,
or **Archive** to scroll the full timeline.

### Reading on a laptop

On a wide screen, the table of contents pins itself to the right side of the
article and highlights the section you're currently reading.

### Reading on a phone

On narrow screens, the same outline collapses to a "Contents" disclosure box
right under the title — tap to expand.

## What's next

More posts, eventually. For now: hello, world.
